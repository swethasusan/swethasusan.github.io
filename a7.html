Electrical Engineering Degree Electrical engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the design, development and maintenance of electrical systems and their components, ensuring their quality, safety, reliability and sustainability. It can involve the design manufacturing of electrical equipment used in different sectors such as construction and building maintenance, transportation, and the production and distribution of power throughout an area
